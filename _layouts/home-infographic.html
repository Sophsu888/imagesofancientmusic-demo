About the Images of Ancient Music Database

A Collection from the WashU Department of Classics. This database includes photos of over 800 extant instruments and works of art showing the music and dance of ancient Greece and Rome, as well as other images related to the ancient Mediterranean world and its influence.

This site has organized available information on all entries into navigable pages. The home page contains widgets of information, and additional information can be found by navigating to each major page.
