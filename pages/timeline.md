## Using the Timeline
The timeline can be navigated by flipping through entries using the arrows on the left and right, or directly scrolling on the timeline itself. Upon opening the page, the starting entry is in 730 BC due to higher frequencies of entries beginning by this century, but there are entries occuring before this year that can be found scrolling left.

## About the Dates
Dates in this timeline were selected using the earliest recorded date or period on an entry. Understandably, some entries do not have any date data, so the timeline is not fully representative of the data. This tool simply serves to visualize the available data we do have. Please refer to full entry page for date ranges, when applicable.
